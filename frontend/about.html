About Us

Protecting Job Seekers with AI

JobGuard AI uses advanced deep learning and natural language processing to detect fraudulent job postings, keeping you safe from scams.

Our Mission

Job scams are a growing threat in the digital age. Every year, millions of job seekers fall victim to fraudulent postings that steal personal information, demand upfront payments, or waste valuable time.

JobGuard AI was built to solve this problem. Our AI-powered system analyzes job postings in real-time, detecting patterns of fraud that humans might miss. We combine deep neural networks with rule-based red flag detection to provide comprehensive protection.
