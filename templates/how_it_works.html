How It Works

AI-Powered Job Verification

Our system combines deep learning with rule-based analysis to provide comprehensive fake job detection in seconds.

Detection Pipeline

From input to verdict — here's what happens behind the scenes

1

Submit Job Posting

Paste a job URL from LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, or Internshala — or paste the full job description text directly.

Data Extraction

If a URL is provided, our web scraper automatically extracts job title, company name, location, salary, requirements, and description from the portal page.

Company

3

Feature Engineering

The system extracts 14 engineered features from the text, measuring linguistic quality, professionalism, and suspicious patterns.

Text Length & Word Count Short descriptions are suspicious
Spelling & Grammar Score Fake jobs have more errors
Readability (Flesch-Kincaid) Professional writing has consistent readability
Sentiment Analysis Overly positive language is a red flag
Lexical Diversity Repetitive text signals low effort
Domain & Company Verification Checks company website legitimacy
4

AI Model Prediction

Features are fed into a Deep Neural Network (4 hidden layers, 439K parameters) along with TF-IDF text vectors. The model outputs a fake probability score from 0 to 1.

500 TF-IDF Features
14 Engineered Features
514 Total Inputs
5

Red Flag Detection

A parallel rule-based engine scans for 14+ specific red flag patterns that are commonly found in fraudulent job postings.

Urgency language
Money requests
Free email domains
Vague descriptions
No experience needed
Unrealistic salary
Personal info requests
Suspicious URLs
6

Combined Verdict

The AI prediction score and red flag analysis are combined to produce a final confidence score with a severity level — helping you make an informed decision.

0-30% Low Risk — Likely Safe
31-60% Moderate — Proceed with Caution
61-85% High Risk — Likely Fake
86-100% Critical — Almost Certainly Fake
